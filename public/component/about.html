Revolutionizing the World : How

Linus Torvalds

Linux, a computer operating system, was created in the early 1990s by Linus Torvalds and the Free Software Foundation. Torvalds developed it as a free alternative to the UNIX operating system. It has since become widely used across various platforms.

Guido van Rossum

Python was created by Guido van Rossum, and first released on February 20,1991. While you may know the python as a large snake, the name of the Python programming language comes from an old BBC television comedy sketch series called Monty Python's Flying Circus.

James Gosling

James Gosling, the lead designer and founder of Java, was born in 1955. In 2004, he was recognized by the Academy of Engineering for his work on Java and window systems. He led the Green Team, which started the Java project at Sun Microsystems in 1991.

Tim Berners-Lee

Tim Berners-Lee invented the World Wide Web in 1989 while working at CERN. He proposed a "universal linked information system" using concepts like hypertext, HTTP, and URLs. This innovation transformed global communication and the way we access information. .

Bill Gates

Bill Gates co-founded Microsoft, making personal computers accessible worldwide. Later, he shifted to philanthropy, focusing on global health, education, and climate change. His work revolutionized technology and positively impacted millions of lives globally.

7. भविष्य को आकार देना :- शिक्षा समय के साथ विकसित होती है और समकालीन जरूरतों और भविष्य की आकांक्षाओं को पूरा करने के लिए सिस्टम, प्लेटफ़ॉर्म, उत्पाद, मशीन और वातावरण का निर्माण करती है।
